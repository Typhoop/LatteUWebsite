What is LatteU?

LatteU is a initiative to revive and restore, many old apps that the Wii U has in store. Some of these apps are still even in the Wii U's source code! (Isn't that neat?) Our current focus right now is a very old app on the Wii U, TVii!

  • TVii was a TV guide tool that allowed you to search between all of your favorite movies, and tv shows! It was eventually shut down in August of 2015. Although that isn't gonna stop us from reviving it!
  • Another neat feature of TVii is TvTag! TvTag was a service that interacted with miiverse, in such a way that it allowed you to comment on tv shows, doodle on images of media, and predict what may happen in that particular piece of media!

    • Is LatteU Finished?
